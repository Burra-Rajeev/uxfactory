The Nokia 3310 had a revival, sporting a fresh version equipped with basic web browsing, a colourful colourful screen and even a camera. Despite this, it still retained our favourite features from the original 3310, including the iconic design, super-long bettery life and even an updated version of Snake. Needless to say, it stole the show at the Mobile World Congress (MWC) tech expo and was one of the biggest hits of the year.
